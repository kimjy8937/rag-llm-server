# Billing & Plans

Plans:
- Free: up to 3 projects per workspace.
- Pro: unlimited projects, advanced permissions, 12 USD per user/month.
- Team: unlimited projects, SSO support, 20 USD per user/month.

Payment methods:
- Credit card is supported.
- Bank transfer is not supported.

Invoices:
- Invoices are generated automatically after a successful payment.
- Download invoices from Billing > Invoices.

Billing cycle:
- Monthly subscription billing.
- Seats are counted based on active workspace members (excluding Guests).
