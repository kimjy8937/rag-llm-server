# Refund Policy

Trial:
- Pro and Team plans include a 7-day free trial for first-time upgrades.

Refund eligibility:
- Full refund is available within 14 days of the first paid subscription purchase.
- Renewals are not refundable.

Cancellation:
- You can cancel anytime. Your plan remains active until the end of the billing period.

Charge disputes:
- If you believe a charge is incorrect, contact support with the invoice ID.
