# Privacy & Data Retention

Issue data:
- Issue history and comments are retained while the project exists.

Deleted projects:
- Deleted projects can be restored within 7 days.
- After 7 days, they are permanently deleted.

Audit logs:
- Audit logs are retained for 30 days on Pro, 180 days on Team.

Attachments:
- Attachments are stored encrypted at rest.
- Public sharing links are not enabled by default.
